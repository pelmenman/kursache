The country was on fire.
Communal riots had paralyzed most of the state.Reyaz, with the help of a friend, got a fake identity card-his new name was Rakesh-and booked a ticket to Aligarh.
The ticket checker on the train asked for his identification.
Reyaz nervously showed the one he had recently procured.
He seemed satisfied and Reyaz heaved a sigh of relief.
At Aligarh there was none to fear.
"Assalamu alaikum," said Reyaz to ward off a group of enraged people.
The angriest of them, with bloodshot eyes, approached Reyaz and asked for his identity card.
